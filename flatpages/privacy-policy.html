Privacy Policy for Agora (agora.gdn)

1. Introduction

Welcome to Agora! This short and simple Privacy Policy explains how we, a Swiss non-profit association, collect, use, and protect your personal data. By using our website, you agree to the terms of this policy.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Email Addresses: For our newsletter and user logins.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to remember your preferences, and for marketing and tracking purposes.
  • Web Analytics Data: We use Plausible Analytics, which is a privacy-friendly tool that does not use cookies and respects your privacy.
  • Payment and Identity Verification Data: When you make a payment or verify your identity, our trusted partners may collect your information. This can include your name, payment details, pictures, and videos of you and your government IDs.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Manage your user account and provide our services.
  • Send you our newsletter (if you've signed up for it).
  • Improve our website and your user experience.
  • Process payments and verify your identity securely.

4. Data Storage

All your personal data is stored securely in Europe.

5. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your data with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our website and services. These partners include:

  • Plausible: For website analytics.
  • Stripe and Datatrans: For payment processing.
  • Stripe and Ondato: For identity verification.

These partners are legally bound to protect your data and are not allowed to use it for any other purpose.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. If you have any questions or want to exercise your rights, please contact us at privacy@agora.gdn.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on our website.
